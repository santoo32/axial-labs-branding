→ CASE STUDY · 01 / 06  ·  ANCHOR LABS  ·  2026  ·  BRAND SYSTEMS

Anchor Labs.

Series B fintech rebrand. Tokenized identity shipped to production in four weeks.

→ CLIENT Anchor Labs
→ ROLE Brand systems lead, principal engineer
→ DURATION 4 weeks · Q1 2026
→ DELIVERABLES Tokens, identity, components, deck
→ 01 / 04   BRIEF

Anchor Labs needed a brand that read as financial precision without looking like another fintech. Their previous identity was a wordmark and a color. Their product was outpacing their visual system.

We were brought in for four weeks. The mandate was clear: ship a tokenized brand and product identity that the team could extend without us.

→ 02 / 04   APPROACH

We started with tokens. Color, type, spacing, motion — all in W3C format, all built through Style Dictionary. Then we layered the visual system on top of that: a custom grotesk wordmark, a single signal accent, and a shape language built around right angles and 45-degree diagonals.

Brand and product team worked from the same repository. Every commit was a token update or a component release. Designers reviewed pull requests. Engineers reviewed motion specs.

  • STRATEGY + VOICE PLAYBOOK
  • TOKENS (W3C FORMAT)
  • WORDMARK + ICON SYSTEM
  • COMPONENT LIBRARY (FIGMA + CODE)
→ 03 / 04   SYSTEM

The system shipped as a private repository: tokens, components, prompts, and the Style Dictionary build pipeline. The Anchor team ran it from day five. By week four, they were extending it without us.
