→ 05 / 06 COLOR RULES
  • 60% surface (black or bone) · 35% neutrals · 5% accent
  • Voltage covers ≤ 5% of pixels in any composition
  • Voltage is a signal, not a paint — use only on black backgrounds
  • voltage-dim (#9DC23A) required for voltage on bone surfaces
  • Body text contrast: AA minimum (4.5:1), AAA (7:1) for editorial
  • Never rely on color alone — pair with text or iconography
  • Alternate accents (ion, plasma, mercury) for seasonal or sub-brand moments
  • All gradients use approved palette only — no custom intermediates
